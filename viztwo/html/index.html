Funding by City

Which cities are the source of the most funds on Bootloader, and how are these funds distributed among categories? This visualization shows these statistics. By default, only the top 5 donations by dollar amount from each city are shown for cleanliness, but the size of each city's bubble represents its relative size of dollars given over the dataset. While not very informative in this dataset, the colors of the cities are based on a weighted average of the donations (e.g. a city that only had technology donations would be pink). Hovering over a category hides all other category's lines, allowing for easier viewing of the biggest donations. Clicking on a city permanently shows only those lines until reset is clicked. Hovering or clicking a city shows all of that city's donations.
